Color Communications LTD. Castlerea, IRL

Color Communications Europe has two physical locations - The main manufacturing facility is located in Castlerea, IRL, and our sales and business services team based out of The Netherlands. Together these facilities work to offer our full range of color services and merchandising products to the European market. CCI's local presence in Europe helps to facilitate a steady flow of worldwide color knowledge and expertise. Benefits of working with our European locations include:

  • Member of the EU with free access to the European market.
  • No trade barriers. No duty or tariffs.
  • Efficient transportation links to the European mainland.
  • Positioned for ideal coverage and optimal access to the color market in Europe.
