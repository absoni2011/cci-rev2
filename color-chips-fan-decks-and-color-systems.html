Color Systems

Color is a language, an art, one of the most brilliant forms of expression.

Color is one of the most fulfilling elements in our lives. It can attract your attention, it can change your mood. It speaks to who you are, how you feel, and where you are going.

Color Systems are key to communicating the language of color to the consumer and business markets.

CCI has designed and produced countless color systems, all customized for our customers.

Color Chips

A color sample available at a hardware or paint store, is the best way to assist a consumer in making a color decision.

CCI has mastered, and reinvented this essential color tool over the years. Our color chips and merchandise are the most accurate color merchandising available.

Color Chips have become more user-friendly than ever before with CCI's patented ColorTac™ Peel & Place samples. Read More on ColorTac™...

Fan Decks

CCI is home to some of the most used, most accurate, and innovative fan deck designs. We produce a dynamic two-sided color fan deck, allowing for double the colors in a single deck.

Fan Deck Features:
  • Custom manufactured to client specifications.
  • Ability to produce any finish on a single medium to achieve desired color impact.
  • Efficient in handling customer requests that are new, trend setting, & unique. Please contact us with your idea.
