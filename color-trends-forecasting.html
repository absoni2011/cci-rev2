OUR WORLD OF COLOR

Color defines the world in which we live. Whether in a home or on a commercial truck, an airplane or a deck stain... The surroundings that we know and love, that illustrate our daily lives, are colors strategically designed to showcase the ways color can transform your surroundings and uplift you.

Our team of Color Stylists and Research Teams bring the latest color data and trend information to our clients and the greater color market alike. Developing palettes that are both exciting and functional help make your product sell in the marketplace by inspiring the people who are seeking color.
