OUR VISION

Our vision is to develop and deliver superior products to a worldwide market, using color in ways that generate additional sales for our customers. We aim to provide the full service color experience for each client we work with.

QUALITY IS FIRST: We are committed to the principle that the first priority of our business is meeting or exceeding the customer expectations and requirements.

SUPERIOR CUSTOMER CARE: is our most important asset. To build relationships, we must understand your needs and expectations, and respond to them in a way which leaves you nothing less than delighted.

TEAMWORK: produces superior results. The ability to bring together the specialized skills and knowledge of our production and sales organization combined with a clear understanding of the needs of our customers is essential to our success.

RESEARCH IS KEY: to success. Our conviction is to constantly strive to develop new products and techniques in a spirit of cooperation with our customers in order to satisfy your evolving needs.

DEDICATION TO GLOBAL MARKET: and our vision of the future that includes an organization designed and structured to serve the color influencing needs of an ever-changing global marketplace.

Let CCI Cater to your Color Needs

The color industry is multi-faceted, ever-changing, and vast. It is also an industry that will never fade. We strive to make the world of color specific to your brand, which entails hosting all color services for every customer we work with on location to cater to each customers' needs with ease and efficiency.

Full service color means taking care of our customers from concept to completion of their color merchandising, with all services in-house, and white-glove handling. Our success is our customers' success.
